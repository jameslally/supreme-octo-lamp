Senior Full Stack Developer

We are looking for a Senior Full Stack Developer to join our innovative technology team. This is a full-time position with competitive salary and benefits.

Required Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in full-stack web development
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field
- Proficiency in Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, and React
- Experience with modern web frameworks and libraries
- Strong understanding of database design and SQL
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Knowledge of version control systems (Git)
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices

Preferred Skills:
- Master's degree in Computer Science or related field
- Experience with Node.js and Express.js
- Knowledge of microservices architecture
- Experience with Docker and Kubernetes
- Familiarity with machine learning concepts
- Experience with agile development methodologies
- Knowledge of testing frameworks (Jest, PyTest)
- Experience with monitoring and logging tools

Responsibilities:
- Design and develop scalable web applications
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Mentor junior developers
- Participate in code reviews and technical discussions
- Contribute to architectural decisions
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues
- Stay updated with industry best practices

Soft Skills:
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
- Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities
- Ability to work independently and in teams
- Leadership and mentoring capabilities
- Adaptability to changing requirements
- Strong attention to detail
- Time management and organizational skills

We offer a collaborative work environment, opportunities for professional growth, and the chance to work on cutting-edge technologies. If you are passionate about software development and meet our requirements, we would love to hear from you!
